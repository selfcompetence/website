What is Transcendental Meditation®?

The Transcendental Meditation ® technique is a simple, natural procedure that allows your mind to effortlessly settle inward through quieter levels of thought, until you experience the most silent and peaceful level of your own awareness – pure consciousness, your innermost Self. TM is non-religious and does not require any change in lifestyle or belief. It’s a simple, efficient mental technique practiced twice a day for 20 minutes, comfortably seated.

During the practice the body gains a deep and rejuvenating state of rest and relaxation, qualified as “restful alertness”, that is at the basis of the wide range of benefits produced by the technique.
